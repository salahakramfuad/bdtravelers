GALLERY

  • Thanchi

    Nestled in the heart of the Chittagong Hill Tracts, Thanchi is a picturesque destination known for its stunning landscapes and adventurous trekking trails. Explore the tranquil beauty of the Naf River, witness the unique Amiakhum Waterfall, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of the indigenous communities.

  • Bandarban: Majestic hills, tribal culture, lush greenery—experience nature's grandeur.

    In the Chittagong Hill Tracts, Bandarban unveils majestic hills, tribal heritage, and breathtaking vistas. Immerse yourself in the natural splendor, trek to remote villages, and encounter diverse indigenous cultures amid the lush greenery.

  • Saint Martin: Pristine beaches, azure waters, tranquil vibes—paradise on this serene island.

    Saint Martin, a jewel in the Bay of Bengal, enchants with pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a laid-back atmosphere. Experience tranquility on this serene island, where stunning sunsets paint the sky and the sound of waves creates a perfect coastal escape.

  • Fantasy Kingdom

    Fantasy Kingdom, located in Dhaka, is a thrilling amusement park that promises a magical escape for all ages. With an array of exciting rides, entertaining shows, and vibrant attractions, it offers a day of joy, laughter, and unforgettable memories for families and thrill-seekers alike.

  • Double falls, Bandarban

    Tucked away in the scenic beauty of Bandarban, Double Falls is a natural wonder where two cascading waterfalls converge into a mesmerizing display of nature's power. Surrounded by lush greenery, the falls provide a tranquil setting for visitors seeking a refreshing retreat amid the hills.

  • Sreemangal: Tea haven, lush plantations, serene landscapes, and vibrant culture await.

    Nestled in Bangladesh's northeast, Sreemangal captivates with lush tea estates, vibrant culture, and serene landscapes. Explore the verdant plantations, discover exotic wildlife, and savor the tranquil ambiance that makes it the Tea Capital of Bangladesh.
